Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Thiopoly Mobile App helps pharmacy teams manage inventory quickly and accurately. It makes it easier to scan items, track stock activity, and stay organized across daily workflows. The app supports smooth handling of bins, containers, and connected devices in one place. Built for operational efficiency, it helps teams save time and reduce manual effort.

Information We Collect

Thiopoly does not collect personal information directly from users. The app may process operational data related to inventory management, such as scanned items, container identifiers, and stock activity, in order to provide its functionality.

How Information Is Used

Information processed by the app is used only for operational purposes, including inventory tracking, stock updates, and workflow management within the organization using the application.

Data Sharing

Thiopoly does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data may be transmitted to the organization's backend systems to enable inventory management features.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect operational data handled by the application from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
